Computing \(\nabla\text{Cost}(p)\) is expensive. It is formulated as:
\begin{equation*} \nabla\text{Cost}(p)=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N\nabla C_{x^{\{i\}}}(p) \end{equation*}
Where
\begin{equation*} C_{x^\{i\}}(p) = \frac{1}{2}||y(x^{\{i\}})-a^{[L]}(x^{\{i\}})||_2^2 \end{equation*}
