Definition 3.1.1.

Given a matrix \(A\in\mathbb{R}^{N\times N}\text{,}\)
\begin{align*} \lambda\in\mathbb{C}\text{ is an eigenvalue of }A\amp\iff\exists v\in\mathbb{R}^N \text{ s.t. } Av = \lambda v \\ v\in\mathbb{R}^N\text{ is an eigenvector of }A\amp\iff\exists \lambda\in\mathbb{C} \text{ s.t. } Av = \lambda v \end{align*}
