It follows from the definition of the Gamma Function that
\begin{align*} \Gamma(t+1)\amp=\int_0^\infty x^t e^{-x}\\ \amp=\big[-x^t e^{-x}\big]_0^\infty+\int_0^\infty tx^{t-1}e^{-x}dx\\ \amp=t\Gamma(t) \\ \text{thus, we have that} \\ \Rightarrow\Gamma(t)\amp=(t-1)\Gamma(t-1)=\cdots=(t-1)!\Gamma(1) \end{align*}
