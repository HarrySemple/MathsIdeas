Now we focus on computing partials of the cost function. We fix \(x^{\{i\}}\) and treat \(C\) as a function of weights and biases.
\begin{align*} C =\amp ||y-a^{[L]}||_2^2\\ z^{[l]}:=\amp W^{[l]}a^{[l-1]}+b^{[l]}\\ \delta^{[l]}\in\amp\mathbb{R}\text{ s.t. }\delta_j^{[l]}=\frac{\partial C}{\partial z_j^{[l]}} \end{align*}
Thus, \(a^{[l]}=\sigma(z^{[l]})\) and \(z_j^{[l]}\) is thought of as the weighed input of neuron \(j\) in layer \(l\)
