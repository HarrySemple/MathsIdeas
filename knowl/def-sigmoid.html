Definition 5.1.1.

A common activation function is the sigmoid function, given by
\begin{equation*} \sigma (x)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}} \end{equation*}
for \(x\in\mathbb{R}\text{.}\)
In future, this will be applied to vectors component-wise. That is, for \(z\in\mathbb{R}^n\)
\begin{equation*} \sigma(z)_i=\sigma(z_i)\text{.} \end{equation*}
